The concept

The concept is to create links with other users to eat extra large pizzas to have a good time and pay as less possible. The goal of the concept is to create a new network of friends and contacts. Pizza is a good way to bring people together.

How it works!

Register and add people from your neighborhood or a city that you will visit. Each user can suggest evenings in a selected pizzeria. The site will negotiate discounts in pizzeria who wish to participate in this sharing.
